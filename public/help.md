# **seesome.art Help Guide**

## A curated tracker for the Paris art scene.

Paris hosts hundreds of exhibitions at any given moment. **seesome.art** helps you filter the noise and build a personal roadmap of the shows that actually matter to you.

---

# 🖼️ The Hierarchy of Your Feed

We organize your feed into sections to help you decide what to do today. Within each section, exhibitions are **sorted by closing date**—so the shows ending soonest are always at the top.

* **Must See:** Your absolute priorities. These are the shows you are determined to visit before they close.  
* **Recommended:** Exhibitions at your **Favorite Venues**. If you love a specific museum or gallery, their new exhibitions will automatically appear here. At least until you reclassify them.  
* **Nice to See:** Shows that sound interesting but you’re not ready to commit to. And not ready to ignore.  
* **Ignored:** These disappear from your feed but you can find them under the Ignored filter.  
* **Not Yet Prioritized:** New exhibitions from across Paris land here first, waiting for you to decide their fate. You can treat these, along with Recommended, as you “inbox.”

---

## 📥 Managing the Noise

The goal of **seesome.art** is to focus your efforts. For every new show, you have four choices:

1. **Promote it** to **Must See** if it’s a high priority. Or **Nice to See** if you like.  
2. **Leave it** in **Recommended** if you trust the venue but aren't ready to commit yet.  
3. **Archive it** as **Attended** once you’ve seen it (this saves it to your personal Journal).  
4. **Ignore it** if it’s not for you. This moves the show to your **Ignored** list and hides it from your main feed forever.

You can also decide what the system will classify as **Recommended**. You can favorite or unfavorite venues by clicking the heart. We’ve taken the liberty of marking several venues as favorites to get things started but you’re in control of that list of venues\!

---

## 📝 Your Art Journal

Exhibitions eventually disappear from city maps, but they shouldn't disappear from your memory. When you mark a show as **Attended**, we move it to your **Journal**. Here, you can look back at your "Art Year," keep track of your ratings, and revisit your personal notes long after the exhibition has closed.

---

## 🔑 Simple Access

We use **Magic Links** for a secure, passwordless experience. Enter your email, click the link in your inbox, and your personalized tracker is synced across your phone and desktop.

### **Why the name?**

In a city with hundreds of galleries and museums, you can’t see it all—but you should definitely **see some**.
